PlaYING WITH the golden ratio

Both products will contain an introduction with explanation providing context and pitch for the given purpose and audience, the interactive product may propose to provide tailored introductions for different audiences (think tankers, individual researchers, donors, grassroots/advocacy organizations, journalists). The introduction text or detailed guidelines on the content of the text will be provided by TTF.

Each example will contain a visual part and a brief description, in the offline version a screenshot(s) will be accompanied with a description. In the interactive version, descriptions should be combined with links to the live websites (inside frame) and screen capture(s). These may be combined with interactive technology. videos or interactive captures (in order to pre-empt missing examples in case original websites are temporarily inaccessible). Provider is expected to make a suggestion on whether a selection should be made from the list of projects, or a means of clustering similar projects (mainly in the online product) provider is also expected to propose a creative name(s) for the two products.
